The purpose of the company, is to run and develop software tools to aid fishermen and businesses related to the fishing industry in and around the Northern Sea.

\subsection{Capital Shares}
The company shares are divded according to the following:
Rasmus Christensen: 45\%
Henrik Klarup: 45\%
Frits L. Christensen: 10\%

At future capital increases, the parties hereby agree that they should maintain the current individual ownership shares (9/9/2).

To increase the capital in the company, the owners must agree, no matter the cause of the raise. 

Other capital shares in the Company, acquired by existing capital owners is automatically converted by this agreement.
